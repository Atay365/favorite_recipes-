Japanese BBQ Wings

Description:

Combing my 2 favorite cuisines of Texas BBQ & Asian food make this an amazing chicken wing. In fact, I'm going to go ahead and declare these wings my current favorite chicken wing!

Ingredients:

  • 3 Dozen chicken wings
  • Meat Church Holy Gospel
  • 1 Bottle Bachan's Japanese BBQ Sauce
  • Toasted sesame seeds
  • Diced scallions

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the wings by cutting the flappers from the drums and marinate in bottle of BBQ sauce overnight in the refridgerator.
  2. Remove wings from marinate and season with Holy Gospel and allow to adhere for 5 minutes.
  3. Prepare the smoker to a temperature of 250. Use a smokey wood pellet, such as oak, mequite, or hickory.
  4. Place the wings in the smoker until internal temperture of at least 175.
  5. Once wings reach internal temp, remove them and set grill to 450 for final step.
  6. Grill wings on grill set to 450 for around 1-2 minutes per side. Be careful not to burn the wings.
  7. Remove from grill once they have reached your desired char level and allow to cool before enjoying.
