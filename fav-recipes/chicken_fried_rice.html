Chicken Fried Rice

Description:

Do you love habachi? Cause I do! Here is a chicken fried rice recipe that is on the table ready to eat in 20 minutes. This recipe is perfect as a main course or as a side dish, regardless its an easy family pleaser!

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 Teaspoons sesame oil
  • 4 Teaspoons neutral oil
  • 3/4 Pound chicken breast
  • 5 Tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
  • 4 Tablespoons butter
  • 2 Teaspoons fresh lemon juice
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1/2 Cup diced white onion
  • 1 Cup frozen mixed vegetables
  • 2 Large egss
  • 4 Cups cooked rice
  • Sesame seeds

Instructions:

  1. In large skillet or wok over medium-high heat, warm 1 ½ teaspoons sesame oil and 1 tablespoon (3 teaspoons) neutral oil. Once oils are hot, add chicken pieces. Let chicken cook for 1 minute without stirring.
  2. Add 2 tablespoons soy sauce, 1 tablespoon butter, 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice, salt, and pepper to skillet. Cook until chicken is no longer pink, approximately 1 to 3 minutes. Stir no more than once or twice so that chicken will brown nicely.
  3. Transfer cooked chicken to medium bowl and set aside. Wipe out skillet (or wok) to use again.
  4. Heat 1 teaspoon neutral oil in skillet over medium-high heat. Add diced onion and frozen vegetables. Sauté until onions are almost translucent, approximately 3 minutes.
  5. Move vegetables to one side of skillet. Crack eggs into skillet and scramble with spatula until cooked through.
  6. Add 4 cups cooked rice and 3 tablespoons butter to skillet. Stir frequently and cook 5 minutes, then stir in 3 tablespoons soy sauce and cook 1 additional minute.
  7. Stir in chicken, salt, and pepper. Cook until everything is well combined and warmed through, then portion into bowls, garnish with sesame seeds if desired, and serve warm.
